Red Window Coffee

Bringing Aussie-flair to a window near you

Red Window provides specialty coffee prepared by trained baristas serving locally roasted Blue Bottle coffee, originally from the bay area now roasting in downtown LA. We offer all available Blue Bottle Hayes Valley Espresso variations, New Orleans cold brew and Three Africans single origin blend drip. Pastries from local cake monkey are also available. We pride our selves as a family owned business with an easy going friendly atmosphere and a high level of customer service and satisfaction. We will never serve or sell coffee over fourteen days post roasting to ensure the highest level of coffee quality and taste!
